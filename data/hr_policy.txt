# Employee HR Policy Guide

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year, accrued monthly. Leave requests must be submitted through the employee portal at least two weeks in advance.

### Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. For absences of more than three consecutive days, a doctor's note is required. Please notify your manager as soon as possible on the first day of your absence.

## Work From Home (WFH) Policy
Employees may be eligible for a hybrid work model, with up to two days of remote work per week, subject to manager approval. The company will provide the necessary software, but employees are responsible for a stable internet connection. All company security protocols must be followed when working remotely.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain a professional and respectful work environment. Harassment of any kind is not tolerated. Please refer to the detailed Code of Conduct document for specific guidelines on ethics and professional behavior.
